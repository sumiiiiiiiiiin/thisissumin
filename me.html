Hi! I’m Sumin. My journey began in engineering, where I learned to approach problems step by step. Over time, I found myself more drawn to people than formulas, which led me to pursue design.

I gained hands-on experience at a web agency producing online magazines, where I focused on digital content and user-centered design. Wanting to deepen my skills and creativity, I moved from Korea to Canada to study Interaction Design at George Brown College.

So, what's my design strength?
I’m strong at finding creative solutions through structured thinking. I enjoy breaking down complex problems and exploring different angles to design experiences .

I believe good design goes beyond visuals — it’s about understanding people. As a UX/UI designer, I aim to create clear, intuitive, and engaging experiences.
